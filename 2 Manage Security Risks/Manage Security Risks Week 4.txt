Manage Security Risks Week 4

	Playbooks
		incident resposne playbooks
			an organizations quick attempt to identify an attack, contain the damage, and correct the effects of the security breach
			1.) Preparation
				document procedures, plans, educating users
			2.) Detection and Analysis
				events using defined processes and technology
			3.) Containment
				prevent further damage
			4.) Eradication and Recovery
				complete removal of incidents artifacts to return to normal operations
					remove malicious code/ mitigate vulnerabilities
					back to a secure state (it restoration)
			5.) Post incident Activity
				documenting incident
				informing leadership
				apply lessons learned for future incidents
			6.) Coordination
				reporting incidents
					ensures compliance
					coordinated response and resolution
		Updates are often made if:
				A failure is identified, such as an oversight in the outlined policies and procedures, or in the playbook itself. 
				There is a change in industry standards, such as changes in laws or regulatory compliance.
				The cybersecurity landscape changes due to evolving threat actor tactics and techniques.
